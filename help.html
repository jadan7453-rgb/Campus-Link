Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our community

Creating a Campus Link account is completely free and takes less than 2 minutes! Simply click the Sign Up button in the top navigation, enter your full name, student email address, select your university, and create a password. Once you verify your email, you'll have full access to all Campus Link features including resources, internships, networking, and more.

Yes! Campus Link is 100% free for all university students. You can access academic resources, browse internships and scholarships, connect with other students, join study groups, and use all core platform features at no cost. We believe every student deserves equal access to opportunities regardless of their financial background.

Visit the Careers page and browse the Internships tab to see all available opportunities. Use the filter sidebar on the left to narrow results by location (Nairobi, Remote, etc.), field of study, salary range, and posting date. You can also use the search bar to find internships by company name, role, or required skills. Click "Apply Now" on any listing to submit your application.

Absolutely! Campus Link encourages students to share their notes and study materials with the community. Go to the Resources page and click the Upload Resource button. You can upload PDFs, Word documents, and PowerPoint files up to 20MB. All uploads are reviewed by our moderation team before being published to ensure quality and appropriateness.

The Networking page lets you browse student profiles from universities across Kenya. Click Connect on any profile to send a connection request. Once accepted, you can message each other directly. You can also join academic communities (like the CS Hub or Business Network), participate in discussion forums, join study groups, and RSVP to events — all in one place.

Yes. Every scholarship listed on Campus Link is manually verified by our team before being published. We check that the provider is a legitimate organization, the application process is transparent, and there are no hidden fees. We work directly with companies, foundations, and government bodies to source authentic scholarship opportunities. If you ever suspect a fraudulent listing, please report it immediately using the report button.

Campus Link has a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, spam, and inappropriate content. To report any content, click the three-dot menu (⋮) on any post, resource, profile, or message and select "Report". Alternatively, email us directly at report@campuslink.co.ke. All reports are handled confidentially and our team responds within 24 hours.

Companies and recruiters can create a free recruiter account on Campus Link. Visit our Companies page and click Create Recruiter Account. Once verified, you can post internship and job listings, search student profiles by skills and university, and engage directly with top student talent. For enterprise partnerships, contact us at partners@campuslink.co.ke.
