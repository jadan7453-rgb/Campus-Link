Our Story

Campus Link was founded in 2022 by a group of Kenyan university students who experienced firsthand the disconnection between university life and the professional world. As students at top Kenyan institutions, we found ourselves struggling to access academic resources, discover internship opportunities, and build meaningful professional networks.

We saw brilliant peers with tremendous potential being held back not by ability, but by lack of access — to the right resources, the right connections, and the right opportunities. We knew something had to change.

Today, Campus Link serves over 10,000 students across 50+ universities in Kenya, and we're rapidly expanding across Africa. Our mission remains simple: give every student the tools, connections, and opportunities they need to succeed.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by a clear purpose and an ambitious vision for the future

Our Mission

To democratize access to academic resources, career opportunities, and professional networks for every university student in Kenya and Africa. We believe that where you come from should never limit where you can go.

Our Vision

A future where every student, regardless of their background or institution, has equal access to the tools and connections needed to achieve their full potential. A continent where talent — not circumstance — determines success.
